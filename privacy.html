Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  •  We take student data privacy very seriously

1 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how SparkEdge School ERP handles information entered by users. SparkEdge is designed with a privacy-first architecture — your school's data never passes through our servers.

🔒 SparkEdge does NOT collect, access, or store any personal data from students, teachers, or staff. All data remains within your school's own storage (browser localStorage or your own Firebase account).

3 Firebase (Optional Cloud Sync)

If you configure Firebase for cloud sync, your data is stored in your own Firebase project under your own Google account. SparkEdge has no access to this Firebase project.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Please review:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
Your Firebase Realtime Database rules should be configured to restrict unauthorized access. The default rules provided in setup allow read/write — tighten these for production use with sensitive data.

4 Student Data & DPDP Compliance

As a school administrator using SparkEdge, you are the Data Fiduciary under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023. Your responsibilities include:

  • Obtaining appropriate consent before collecting student/guardian data
  • Using collected data only for legitimate educational purposes
  • Ensuring student Aadhaar numbers and sensitive data are stored securely
  • Providing data deletion on request from students/guardians
  • Not sharing student data with unauthorized third parties

SparkEdge does not process this data on your behalf and therefore is not a Data Processor under DPDPA. You maintain full control and responsibility.

5 Cookies & Local Storage

SparkEdge uses the following browser storage mechanisms:

  • localStorage — Stores all school data locally in the browser. Persists until manually cleared.
  • sessionStorage — Stores login session. Automatically cleared when the browser tab is closed.
  • No cookies — SparkEdge does not set any HTTP cookies or tracking cookies.

6 Student Photo Privacy

Student and teacher photos uploaded to the system are:

  • Compressed to 200×200px and stored as base64 within your school's data
  • Never transmitted to SparkEdge servers
  • Only accessible to users logged into the ERP system
  • Deletable by removing or editing the student/teacher record

7 Security Recommendations

To protect your school's data, SparkEdge recommends:

  • Changing all default passwords before going live
  • Using HTTPS (GitHub Pages provides this automatically)
  • Restricting Firebase rules to authenticated access in production
  • Regular data exports stored in secure, access-controlled locations
  • Deactivating accounts immediately when staff leave
  • Not sharing the owner password with any staff member

8 Contact & Data Requests

Since SparkEdge does not hold any of your school's data, data access or deletion requests should be handled directly by your school administration within the ERP system.
